Treasures of the Library

The University of Manchester's Special Collections of rare books, manuscripts, archives, visual materials and maps are an outstanding research resource across many disciplines.

They span 5,000 years and six continents, and include almost every format ever used for written communication - from papyrus to pixels.

Many of the items derive from the superlative collections purchased by Enriqueta Rylands for the magnificent library she founded as a memorial to her husband John. But the collections continue to expand, through purchase, gift and bequest, as we respond to new areas of research and build upon existing strengths.
