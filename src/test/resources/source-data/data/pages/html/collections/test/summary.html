The University of Manchester Library holds over 400 Hebrew manuscripts. They include de luxe decorated manuscripts such as the famous Rylands Haggadah; rare and unique items which illuminate marginal forms of Judaism; a collection of Ketubbot (marriage contracts); and amulets and other magical texts assembled by Moses Gaster.
